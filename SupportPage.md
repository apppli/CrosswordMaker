## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1) How do I create a puzzle?
Open the app in Editor mode, tap cells to enter letters, and use block/unblock to shape the grid.

### 2) How do I add clues?
Open Settings (gear icon), then enter Across and Down clues. Clue numbers are generated automatically.

### 3) How do I test-solve my puzzle?
Switch to Play mode and fill in answers. Tap Check to validate your entries.

### 4) How do I save my puzzle?
Open Settings and use Save to store puzzle data locally on your device.

### 5) How do I move puzzles between devices?
Use Export to create a JSON file and Import on another device.

### 6) Why can’t I see my old puzzle?
If local data was removed from the device or app, it may no longer be available. Keep backups with Export.

## Troubleshooting
- Force close and reopen the app.
- Restart your device.
- Ensure iOS is up to date.
- Update to the latest app version.

If the issue continues, email support with:
