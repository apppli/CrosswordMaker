# Privacy Policy - Crosswords Maker

Last updated: March 9, 2026

## 1. Overview
Crossword Maker ("the App") is designed to let users create and play crossword puzzles on their own device.

## 2. Data We Collect
We do not collect personal data from users through the app.

## 3. Data Storage
Puzzle data created in the app is stored locally on the user's device.

If a user chooses to export a puzzle, the app writes a JSON file to a user-selected location through Apple's document picker.

## 4. Tracking and Analytics
We do not track users across apps or websites.
We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.
We do not use third-party analytics SDKs.

## 5. Accounts
The app does not require account creation or sign-in.

## 6. Children's Privacy
The app is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Third-Party Services
The app may use Apple-provided system frameworks only (for file selection, storage, and app functionality).

## 8. Security
Because data is stored locally by default, users control their puzzle files. Users should manage exported files according to their own security preferences.

## 9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
